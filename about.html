About Securinets
Securinets is the first Tunisian Association to base all its activities around Cyber Security. Starting in 2004 at INSAT as a Club, it counted at that time only 5 members. Today, Securinets is proud to have over 3000 members, 250 of whom are active members of Securinets INSAT and attend our events regularly at INSAT. The Securinets family does not only have a branch at INSAT, but also thirteen other branches in different universites and a wide network of experts formed by former members who are now present in the field of cyber security all over the world.

About Securinets TEK-UP
Securinets TEK-UP is one of the thirteen branches founded in 2022 at TEK-UP University.
Our Goals
Securinets seeks through its activities to:
 • Enrich the culture of its members in terms of cyber-security.
 • Raising awareness of the dangers of cyber attacks.
 • Study and simulate the attacks in order to be able to protect.
 • To popularize the use of computer security tools.
 • Inculcate the culture of researching system flaws and vulnerabilities through challenges developed by our teams.
 • Strengthen the spirit of teamwork and the sharing of knowledge .
 • Establish contacts with the professional world .
